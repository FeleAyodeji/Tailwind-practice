The Digital Divide in Numbers

Over 60% of young Nigerians lack access to a computer or reliable internet.

Only 15% of students receive formal digital skills training in school.

Unemployment among Nigerian youth stands at over 30%, partly due to lack of digital literacy.

Cyber risks are rising, yet many young people lack digital safety education.
